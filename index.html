About Me

Personal Details

I am currently working as a Research Assistant at the Institute of Information Technology, University of Dhaka (DU), under the supervision of Dr. Emon Kumar Dey. I completed my Master's in Software Engineering at DU and received a fellowship grant from the ICT Ministry of Bangladesh for my thesis. Prior to that, I completed my Bachelor of Science in Computer Engineering from the American International University Bangladesh. My research interests lie in computer vision, machine learning, and deep learning, with a particular focus on point cloud segmentation, human activity recognition, and object detection. Before starting my master's, I worked as an Embedded Systems Engineer at STATA IT Limited and as a Machine Learning Intern at Pioneer Alpha. I am passionate about developing complex applications that address real-world problems and make a significant impact on millions of users.
